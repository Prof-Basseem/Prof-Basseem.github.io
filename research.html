Research Portfolio

Exploring the Frontiers of Applied Organic Chemistry

Research Areas

Chemosensors for Chemical Detection

Development of advanced chemosensors for detecting various chemical species using fluorescence-based probes and molecular recognition principles.

AI & Machine Learning in Chemistry

Integrating artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques for chemical analysis, prediction, and discovery of new compounds.

Supramolecular Interactions

Investigating non-covalent interactions between molecules to design new materials with specific properties and functions.

Polymer-based Gas Sensors

Development of polymer-based gas sensors through Suzuki coupling reactions for environmental monitoring applications.

Nanomaterials for Catalysis

Synthesis and characterization of nanomaterials for catalytic applications and wastewater treatment technologies.

Photocatalysis using Nanocomposites

Research on photocatalytic processes using advanced nanocomposite materials for environmental remediation.

Electrochemical Studies

Investigation of electrochemical properties of organic compounds and their applications in energy storage systems.

Heterocyclic Compounds

Synthesis and characterization of heterocyclic compounds for material science and pharmaceutical applications.
