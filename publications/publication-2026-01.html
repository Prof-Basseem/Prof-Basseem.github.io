Multi-targeting Fluorescence Probe Based on Coumarin Receptors for Recognition of Cyanide and Picric Acid

Publication Details

Authors:
Reda M. El-Shishtawy, Mahmoud A. Hussein, Mohamed Abdel Salam, Abdullah Y. Obaid, Mervat El-Sedik, Mahmoud Basseem I. Mohamed, Tarek Aysha
Journal: Microchemical Journal
Article Number: 117017
Year: 2026
Citation: Microchemical Journal, 2026, 117017

This work reports a multi-targeting fluorescence probe built on coumarin receptors for selective recognition of two analytically important species: cyanide and picric acid.

The study highlights the value of coumarin-based molecular design in developing responsive sensing platforms with practical relevance for chemical detection, optical signaling, and analytical applications.
