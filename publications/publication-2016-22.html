Synthesis of Novel Acid Dyes with Coumarin Moiety and Their Utilization for Dyeing Wool and Silk Fabrics

Publication Details

Authors:
Mahmoud S. Bashandy, Fatma A. Mohamed, Mohamed M. El-Molla, Mahmoud Basseem I. Mohamed, Ahmed H. Bedair
Journal: Open Journal of Medicinal Chemistry
Volume: 6
Pages: 18â€“35
Year: 2016

Abstract

This research focuses on the synthesis of novel acid dyes incorporating coumarin moiety for textile applications. The study explores the application of these newly synthesized dyes for dyeing wool and silk fabrics, evaluating their coloring properties, fastness characteristics, and practical utility in the textile industry.

Key Contributions

  • Development of novel coumarin-based acid dyes
  • Comprehensive evaluation of dyeing properties
  • Application studies on wool and silk fabrics
  • Assessment of color fastness and durability
  • Optimization of dyeing conditions

Research Significance

This work contributes to the field of textile chemistry by introducing new synthetic pathways for coumarin-based dyes and demonstrating their practical applications in the dyeing of natural fibers. The research provides valuable insights for the textile industry regarding sustainable and effective dyeing solutions.
