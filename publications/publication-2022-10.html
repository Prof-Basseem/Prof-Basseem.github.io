Publication #10 2022

New Stilbene-Biscarbothioamide Based Colorimetric Chemosensor and Turn on Fluorescent Probe for Recognition of Hg²⁺ Cation

Publication Details

Authors:
Mahmoud Basseem I. Mohamed, Mervat S. El-Sedik, Yehya A. Youssef, Tarek S. Aysha
Journal: Journal of Photochemistry and Photobiology A: Chemistry
Volume: 433
Article: 114206
Year: 2022
DOI: 10.1016/j.jphotochem.2022.114206
View Full Publication

Abstract

This work presents the development of a novel stilbene-biscarbothioamide based chemosensor for the selective detection of mercury(II) ions through colorimetric and fluorescence mechanisms. The sensor demonstrates excellent selectivity and sensitivity for Hg²⁺ recognition in aqueous media.
