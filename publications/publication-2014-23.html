Publication #23 2014

Synthesis of Several Newly Acid Dyes and their Application in Textile Dyeing

Authors: Fatma A. Mohamed, Mahmoud S. Bashandy, H. Abd El-Wahab, Mahmoud Basseem I. Mohamed, Mohamed M. El-Molla, Ahmed H. Bedair
Journal: Int. J. Adv. Res.
Year: 2014
Volume & Pages: 2, 248â€“260
URL: journalijar.com/article/2106/

Abstract

This research focuses on the synthesis of several newly developed acid dyes and their practical application in textile dyeing processes. The study explores the chemical synthesis routes, characterization of the synthesized dyes, and their performance evaluation in textile applications. The work contributes to the development of new coloring agents for the textile industry with improved properties and performance characteristics.

Key Features

  • Novel acid dye synthesis
  • Comprehensive characterization methods
  • Textile dyeing applications
  • Performance evaluation studies
  • Industrial application potential
  • Color fastness testing
  • Chemical stability analysis

Research Scope

The research encompasses the complete development cycle from synthesis to application, including detailed analysis of the dye properties, optimization of dyeing conditions, and evaluation of the final textile products. This comprehensive approach ensures practical relevance and industrial applicability of the developed dyes.

Industrial Significance

This work has significant implications for the textile industry by providing new dye options with potentially improved performance characteristics. The research contributes to the ongoing development of more efficient and sustainable dyeing processes.
