Publication #16 2021

Multi-functional colorimetric chemosensor for naked eye recognition of Cu2+, Zn2+ and Co2+ using new hybrid azo-pyrazole/pyrrolinone ester hydrazone dye

Authors: Tarek S. Aysha, Mahmoud Basseem I. Mohamed, Mervat S. El-Sedik and Yehya A. Youssef
Journal: Dyes and Pigments
Year: 2021
Volume & Pages: 196, 109795
DOI: 10.1016/j.dyepig.2021.109795

Abstract

This publication presents the development of a multi-functional colorimetric chemosensor based on a new hybrid azo-pyrazole/pyrrolinone ester hydrazone dye. The sensor demonstrates excellent selectivity and sensitivity for the naked eye recognition of copper(II), zinc(II), and cobalt(II) ions. The research contributes to the field of analytical chemistry by providing a simple, cost-effective method for metal ion detection.

Key Features

  • Multi-functional chemosensor design
  • Naked eye detection capability
  • Selective recognition of Cu²⁺, Zn²⁺, and Co²⁺
  • Hybrid azo-pyrazole/pyrrolinone structure
  • Colorimetric response analysis
