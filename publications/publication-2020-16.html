Publication #17 2020

A highly sensitive colorimetric chemosensor and turn on fluorescence probe for pH monitoring and bio-imaging application in living cells

Publication Details

Authors:
Mahmoud Basseem I. Mohamed, Tarek S. Aysha, Taha M. Elmorsi, Ahmed H. Bedair
Journal: Journal of Fluorescence
Volume: 30
Pages: 601â€“612
Year: 2020

Abstract

This study presents the development of a highly sensitive dual-mode chemosensor capable of both colorimetric and fluorescence detection for pH monitoring. The sensor demonstrates excellent performance in bio-imaging applications within living cell systems, providing real-time pH monitoring capabilities with high sensitivity and selectivity.

Key Features

  • Dual colorimetric and fluorescence detection modes
  • High sensitivity for pH monitoring
  • Bio-compatible for living cell applications
  • Real-time imaging capabilities
  • Excellent selectivity and response time
